There are three kinds of Arabic

  1. Spoken Arabic
    • It is dialetical
    • Each Arab state has its own kind of Arabic
    • When speaking with someone
    • When ordering food
    • When watching tv or movies
  2. Formal Arabic
    • It is used in formal writing
  3. Old Arabic
    • It is the language of the Quran
    • It died in the desert years ago
    • It has been preserved along with the Quran and the Hadith

The Arabic Language

  • The Arabs had Greek, Roman, and Persian neigbors
  • But everyone had left the Arabs alone
  • The Arabs had nothing and oil was not discovered yet
  • When you are left alone then you talk to each other
  • When there is outside language then the original language dies
  • When there is not outside influence the original language is preserved
  • Then your language becomes more and more refined
  • Every nation has something to be proud of
  • The Arabs figured they had the world's best language
  • So they preserved the language and made it better and better

The Example of an Arab Poet

  • There was an Arab poet who was poor and married
  • One day his wife complained to him about the sustenance
  • So he replied to her with poetry
  • He said heavy rain is at war with a house on a hill
  • Heavy Rain also means sustenace from Allah
  • So he is saying lots of wealth comes to me, but I am such a high person
  • When wealth comes it leaves me and goes down to the people below in the flood and the mud
