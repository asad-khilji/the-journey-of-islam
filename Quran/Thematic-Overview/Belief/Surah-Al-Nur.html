Surah Al Nur

Ayah 35

  • Allah is the light of the heavens and the earth
  • The example of His light is like a niche within which is a lamp
  • The lamp is within a glass
  • The glass as if it were a pearly white star lit from a blessed olive tree
  • Neither of the east or the west
  • Whose oil would amost glow even if untouched by fire,
  • Light upon Light
  • Allah guides to His light whom He wills
  • And Allah presents examples to the people
  • And Allah is knowing of all things

Ayah 36

  • Such niches are in houses which Allah has ordered to be raised
  • And that His name be mentioned therein
  • Exalting Him within them in the morning and in the evening

Ayah 37

  • True Men are those whom neither commerce nor sale distracts them from the remebrance of Allah
  • And performance of prayer and giving zakah
  • They fear a day in which the hearts and eyes will fearfully turn about

Ayah 38

  • That Allah may reward them according to the best of what they did
  • And increase them from His bounty
  • And Allah gives provision to whom He wills without account

Ayah 39

  • But those who disbelieved their deeds are like a miraj in a lowland
  • In which a thristy one thinks is water until when he comes to it, he finds it is nothing
  • But he finds Allah before him
  • Allah will pay him in full his dues
  • And Allah is swift in account

Ayah 40

  • Or they are like darknesses within an unfathomable sea
  • Which is covered by waves upon which are waves over which are clouds
  • Darkness upon darkness
  • When one puts out his hand, he can hardly see it
  • And he to whom Allah has not granted light, for him there is no light
