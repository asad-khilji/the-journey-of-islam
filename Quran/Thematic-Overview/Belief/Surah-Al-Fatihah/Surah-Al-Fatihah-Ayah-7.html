Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 7

غَيْرِ الْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا الضَّالِّينَ

But not the Path of those who had wrath upon them

  • Those who had beneficial knowledge but did not act upon it
  • These people are found in the past, present, and future
  • The Quran gives us a case study of these people through the stories of the Jews, but not all Jews
  • Allah, the angels, and the believers are angry at them
  • These people lead other people to become lost because they look to them for knowledge
  • They increase more wrongdoing in the world

Nor of the lost

  • Those who did not have beneficial knowledge and acted wrongly
  • These people are found in the past, present, and future
  • The Quran gives us a case study of these through the stories of the Christians, but not all Christians
  • The guidance is there available for them but they choose not to know

Example

  • I have two sons Imad and Waleed
  • Both Imad and Waleed are looking at the cookies
  • I am about to go upstairs to take a phone call
  • I leave Imad in charge and tell him not to eat the cookies
  • Waleed cannot eat them either, but i left Imad in charge and told him not to eat it
  • When I come back I see Imad and Waleed eating the cookies
  • I am angry at Imad because I told him not to eat the cookies
  • I am not that angry at Waleed, but I still hold him responsible
  • Waleed should have asked me not Imad
