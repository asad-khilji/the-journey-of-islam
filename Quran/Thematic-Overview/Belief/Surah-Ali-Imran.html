Surah Ali Imran

Ayah 190

  • Indeed in the creation of the heavens and the earth
  • And the alternation of the night and the day
  • Are signs for those of understanding

Ayah 191

  • Who remember Allah while standing, sitting, or lying on their sides
  • And those who give thought to the creation of the heavens and the earth
  • Saying Our Master You did not create this aimlessly
  • How Perfect are You above such a thing
  • Then protect us from the punishment of the fire

Ayah 192

  • Our Master indeed whoever You admit to the fire
  • You have disgraced him
  • And for the wrongdoers
  • There are no helpers

Ayah 193

  • Our Master indeed we have heard a caller calling to faith
  • Saying believe in your Master and we have believed
  • Our Master forgive us and remove from us our misdeeds
  • And cause us to die with the righteous

Ayah 194

  • Our Master grant us what You promised us through Your messengers
  • And do not disgrace us on the Day of Resurrection
  • Indeed You do not fail in Your promise

Ayah 195

  • And their Master responded to them
  • Never will I allow to be lost the work of any among you whether male or female
  • So those who emigrated or were evicted fron their homes
  • Or were harmed in My cause or killed
  • I will surely remove from them their misdeeds
  • And I will surely admit them to gardens beneath which rivers flow
  • As a reward from Allah and Allah has with Him the best reward

Ayah 196

  • Be not deceived by the unihabited movement of the disbelievers throughout the land

Ayah 197

  • It is but a small enjoyment
  • Then their refuge is Hell
  • And wretched is the resting place

Ayah 198

  • But those who feared their Master will have gardens beneath which rivers flow
  • Abiding therein eternally as an accomodation from Allah
  • And that which is with Allah is the best for the righteous

Ayah 199

  • And indeed among the People of the Book are those who believe in Allah
  • And what was revealed to you and what was revealed to them being humbly submissive to Allah
  • They do not exchange the ayat of Allah for a small price
  • those will have their reward with their Master
  • Indeed Allah is swift in account

Ayah 200

  • Believers preserve and endure and remain stationed
  • And fear Allah so that you may be successful
