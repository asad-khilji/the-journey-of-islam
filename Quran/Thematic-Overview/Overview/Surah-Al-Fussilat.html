Surah Al Fussilat

Ayah 30

  • Indeed those who have said ur Master is Allah and remained on a right course
  • The angels will descend upon them saying Do not fear and do not grieve
  • And recieve glad tidings of Paradise of which you were promised

Ayah 31

  • We the angels were you allies in the worldly life and in the Hereafter
  • And you will have therein whatever your sould desire
  • And you will have therein whatever you request or wish

Ayah 32

  • As an accomodation from a Master who is Forgiving, Loving, and Caring

Ayah 33

  • And who is better in speech than one who invites to Allah and does righteousness
  • And the one who says Indeed I am of the Muslims

Ayah 34

  • And not equal are the good deed and the bad deed
  • Repel evil with that which is better
  • And thereupon the one whom between you and him is enmity will become a devouted friend

Ayah 35

  • But none is granted it except those who are patient
  • And none is granted it except the one having a great portion of good

Ayah 36

  • And if there comes to you from Satan an evil suggestion
  • Then seek refuge in Allah
  • Indeed Allah is the Hearing, the Knowing
