Dialogue in the Quran

Surah Al Shu ara

  1. Ta. Sin. Mim
  2. These are the ayat of the clear book
  3. Perhaps you would kill yourself with grief that they will not be believers
  4. If We willed, We could send down to them from the sky a sign for which their necks would remain humbled
  5. And no revelation comes to them anew from Ar Rahman except that they turn away from it
  6. For they have already denied, but there will come to them the news of that which they used to ridicule
  7. Did they not look at the earth how much We have produced therein from every noble kind?
  8. Indeed in that is a sign, but most of them were not to be believers
  9. And indeed Your Master He is the Exalted in Might, The Always Loving and Caring
  10. And mention when Your Master called Musa saying go to the wrongdoing people
  11. The people of the Pharoah, don't they have any fear
  12. He said My Master indeed i fear that they will deny me
  13. And that my chest will tighten and my tongue will not be fluent, so send for Harun
  14. And they have upon me a claim dur to sin, so I fear that they will kill me
  15. Allah said No problem go both of you with Our signs indeed We are with you listening
  16. Go to Pharoah and say We are the Messenger of the Master of all people
  17. And send with us the children of Israel
  18. Pharoah said Did we not raise you among as a child and you remained among us for years of your life
  19. And then you did your deed that you did and you were of the ungrateful
  20. Musa said I did it then when I was of those who were lost
  21. So I fled from you when I feared you then My Master granted me wisdom and prophethood and appointed me as one of the messengers
  22. And this is the favor of which you remind me that you enslaved the Children of Israel
  23. Pharoah said And what Master of the people
  24. Musa said The Master of the heavens and the earth and that which is between them if you should be convinced
  25. Pharoah said to those around him Do you not hear?
  26. Musa said Your Master and the Master of your first forefathers
  27. Pharoah said indeed your messenger who has been sent to you is mad
  28. Musa said Master of the east and the west and that between them if you were to reason
  29. Pharoah said If you take a god other than me I will surely place you among those imprisoned
  30. Musa said Even if I brought you clear proof
  31. Pharoah said then bring your proof if you are truthful
  32. So Musa threw his staff and it became a giant snake
  33. And he drew out his hand thereupon it was white for the observers
  34. Pharoah said to the eminent ones around him Indeed this is a learned magician
  35. He wants to drive you out of your land by his magic, so what do you advise
  36. They said Postpone the matter of him and his brother and send among the cities gatherers
  37. Who will bring you every learned and skilled magician
  38. So the magicians were assembled for the appointment of a well known day
  39. And it was said to the people Will you congregate
  40. That we might follow the magicians if they are the predominant
  41. And when the magicians arrived they said Paharoah Is there indeed for us a reward if we are the predominant
  42. He said yes and indeed you will be of those near to me
  43. Musa said to them throw whatever you will throw
  44. So they threw their ropes and their staffs and said By the might of Pharoah indeed it is we who are predominant
  45. Then Musa threw his staff and at once it devoured what they falsified
  46. So the magicians fell into prostration
  47. They said We have believed in the Master of all people
  48. The Master of Musa and Harun
  49. Pharoah said You believed Musa before I gave you permission. Indeed he is your teacher who has taught you magic but you are going to know. I will surely cut off your hands and your feet on opposites sides and I will crucify you all.
  50. They said No harm Indeed to our Master we will return
  51. Indeed we aspire that our Master will forgive us because we were the first of the believers
  52. And We inspired to Musa Travel by night with My slaves indeed you will be pursued
  53. Then Pharoah sent among the cities gatherers
  54. And said Indeed those are but a small band
  55. And indeed they are enraging us
  56. And indeed we are a cautious society
  57. So we removed them from gardens and springs
  58. And treasures and honorable station
  59. And we caused to inherit it the Children of Israel
  60. So they pursed them at sunrise
  61. And when the two companies saw one another the companions of Musa said Indeed we are to be overtaken
  62. Musa said No Indeed with me is my Master He will guide me
  63. Then We inspired to Musa strike with your staff the sea and it parted and each portion was like a great towering mountain
  64. And We saved Musa and those with him all together
  65. Then We drowned the others
  66. Indeed in that is a sign but most of them were not to be believers
  67. And indeed Your Master He is the exalted in might the always loving and caring
