We in the Quran

Why Allah uses We in the Quran to describe Himself

  • Allah uses the pronouns I, You, We, and He to describe Himself
  • The pronoun We is not used consistently
  • You is only used when we make dua to Allah
  • Allah speaking on His own behalf He uses I, We, and He
  • I and We are close
  • He is far away
  • I is used in extreme anger or extreme love and it is close
  • The pronoun He is formal and distant
  • The pronoun We is used to describe the majesty of Allah
  • It is the royal We
  • The pronoun We is used to describe the grandeur of Allah
  • With water you find manifestation of Allah's gifts
  • We is used for Allah's grand gifts for us
  • The pronoun We is immediately followed by a singular attribute of Allah
