Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 6

صِرَاطَ الَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ

The Path of those whom You have favored

  • The path of those who are relaxed in their walk
  • Allah made the path easy for them
  • They are almost effortless in their movement like cattle walking
  • It is like going up an escalator
  • Those who had beneficial knowledge and acted upon it
  • The result of going up this path is from Allah
  • This ayah is in the past tense so these people are only found in the past
  • The Quran is full of stories of people who already made this journey and Allah told us they passed
  • Every story in the Quran will help you on this path and it is revelant until the day of judgment
  • Their stories are my companions on this Journey

Who are the People of this path?

  1. The Prophets
  2. Those who confirmed truth in the Prophets
  3. The Martyrs
  4. The Righteous
