Surah Al Fatihah - Concluding Remarks

Introduction

  • The Quran is an incredible depiction of balance
  • This beautiful surah is balanced in a way that is humanly impossible

Praise and Thanks

  • If you just have praise then thanks is missing
  • If you just have thanks then praise is missing
  • Hamd includes both praise and thanks
  • Hamd is balanced

Ar Rahman Ar Raheem and Malik Yom Ad Deen

  • If you just say Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem
  • Then people will take advantage of the the love and care of Allah and fall into sin
  • If you just say Malik Yom Ad Deen
  • Then people will be scared of Allah and lose hope and fall into sin

The Three Parts of the Fatihah

  • The First three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah
  • The first half of the middle ayah is about Allah
  • The second half of the middle ayah is about us
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah is about us

Nouns and Verbs

  • The First three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah and it is noun based
  • Nouns are permanent and Allah is permanent
  • The first half of the middle ayah is about Allah and it is noun based
  • The second half of the middle ayah is about us and it is verb based
  • Verbs are temporary and we are temporary
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah are verb based because it is about us

Balance in Fatihah

  • The First three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah and the first half of the middle ayah concludes it
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah is about us and the second half of the middle ayah introduces it

Knowledge and Action

  • The first three ayat of the Fatihah is about knowledge
  • The middle ayah is about action
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah is about acting upon knowledge and not acting upon it

Al Alameen

  • Allah mentions in the first ayah that He is Master of all Nations and People
  • All Nations and People are summed up into three groups
    1. Those who had knowledge and acted upon it and those whom Allah had favored
    2. Those who had knowledge but did not act upon it and those who were the recipients of rage
    3. Those who had no knowledge and acted wrongly and the lost

Fatihah and Nas

    Surah Al Fatihah Surah Al Nas
    Surah Al Fatihah is the first surah Surah Al Nas is the last surah
    Surah Al Fatihah begins with something positive Surah Al Nas begins with something negative
    Surah Al Fatihah says Rabb ul Alameen Surah Al Nas says Rabb ul Nas
    Surah Al Fatihah says Malik Yom Ad Deen Surah Al Nas says Malik in Nas
    In Fatihah we say You alone we worship In Nas Allah mentions the God of people who the source of worship for people
    Surah Al Fatihah mentions two negative forces those who earned wrath and the lost Surah Al Nas mentions two negative forces from amongst the Jinn and People

Conflicts in Society

  • Social Conflicts
    • The conflict between men and women
    • If you ask Men who should have more rights they will say Men
    • If you ask Women who should have more rights they will say women
    • Because whether you are a man or woman you are biased
    • You will favor one side over the other
    • You cannot help it, it is human nature
    • There has always been a conflict between Men and Women

  • Economic Conflicts
    • The conflicts between employers and employees
    • Employer says more work and less pay
    • Employee says more less work and more pay
    • Employer says less vacation time
    • Employee says more vacation time

  • Political Conflicts
    • The conflict between government and people
    • Government says more taxes from the people
    • People say less taxes from the Government
    • People say less control from the Government
    • Too much freedom could cause corruption in society
    • Government says more control over the people
    • Too much control could cause anarchy and corruption in society

  • Internal Conflicts
    • The conflicts between the body and the soul
    • Your body says more exercise and less sleep
    • Your soul says less exercise and more sleep
    • If you listen to the body then your soul dies
    • If you listen to your soul then your body dies

The Solution to Societies Problems

  • Allah is the One Who created Men and Women
  • Allah is the One Who created Governments and People
  • Allah is the One Who created Employers and Employees
  • Allah is the One Who gave you a body and a soul
  • Allah is the only One Who can resolve all these conflicts in society
  • Allah is the only One Who can create a balance in the society
