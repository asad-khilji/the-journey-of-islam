Surah Al Fatihah Ayah 4

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

Iyyaka Na Budu

  • It is only to You we willingly gives ourselves in slavery and worship
  • We are now engaged in a direct conversation with Allah
  • Allah the most important Being is talking to you and me, What an Honor!
  • We make decisions based on our intellect
  • We make decisions because our heart is compelled towards it
  • We thought about who Allah is and our emotions were shaken
  • We willingly enslaves ourselves to Allah with both heart and mind
  • Surah Al Fatihah is re-starting your relationship with Allah
  • We must ask why we are Muslim over and over again
  • We now made a commitment to Allah to be His slave 24/7
  • Salah is a practical demonstration of slavery to Allah
  • We become better slaves after each salah

Iyyaka Nastain

  • We made a commitment to Allah that we will be His slave 24/7
  • It is a hard commitment and we need help
  • So the next thing we ask is help from Allah
  • We do not ask what we need help in
  • It is because the list is so long that we need help in everything
  • It is also because we are desperate
  • When you are desperate then you are short on words

Example

  • Imagine you are hanging onto a tree about to fall from a cliff
  • You do not say could you please help me, I am about to fall from this cliff and could possibly get injury leading to death
  • Instead you just yell HELP!

The way to ask for help

  • You put in the effort then Allah sends His help
  • If you do not put in the effort, Allah's help will not come
  • There are two extremes
  • Some people say I did everything while other people do nothing
  • Both these extremes are wrong
  • We must put in the effort then ask Allah for help

      • First Scenario

    • You have a flat tire
    • Then you ask for help
    • Someone comes and helps you while you are sitting doing nothing

      Second Scenario

    • You have a flat tire
    • Then you ask for help
    • Someone comes and helps you while you are trying to fix it yourself
  • The second scenario is the right way to ask Allah for help
  • Success = your efforts + results from Allah
  • Allah is asking for your best efforts
  • Results and help is only in the hands of Allah
  • Allah will not give His help to those who do not show effort
