Surah Al Fatihah

1. The Ultimate Praise and Thanks is for Allah, Master of all Nations and People

2. The extremely loving and caring right now, the always extremely loving and caring

3. The owner and the king of the say of judgment

4. You alone we worship and You alone we as for help

5. Guide us on the straight path upwards

6. The path of those whom You have favored

7. But not the path of those who had anger upon them nor of the lost

Surah Al Baqarah

1. Alif Laam Meem

2. That is the Book. There is absolutely no doubt in it. Guidance! for the cautious

3. Who believe in the unseen, establish salah, and, only in what We provided them, they spend

4. And who believe in whatever was sent to you and whatever was sent much before you and in the afterlife they have absolute conviction

5. Those people are commited to guidance from their Master and those people are the reapers of success
