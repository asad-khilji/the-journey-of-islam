Verbal Idioms

Introduction

  • Language has many figures of speech
  • Language is alive and dynamic
  • Language changes a lot overtime
  • Over the years the same word could mean different things
  • Words cannot be taken literally all the time
  • Allah uses verbal idioms or figures of speech in the Quran as well

Examples

  • I gave means to give something like time or money
  • I gave up means I quit
  • Site to a construction worker it is a construction site
  • Site to a web developer it is a web site
  • There is also sight which means to see
  • An accountant has an accounting journal
  • A professor and student has an acedemic journal
  • A teenage girl or storywriter has a personal journal
  • Cool means it is awesome
  • Cool is also the weather
  • What's up means to look up
  • What's up also means how are you

Coolness of the Eyes

  • Shedding tears of joy
  • Finding relief from a storm
  • When your eyes stay in one place and it calms you down
  • We make a dua in Surah Al Furqan Ayah 74 for coolness of the eyes
  • Surah Qasas Ayah 9 Pharoah and his wife experience coolness of the eyes
  • Surah Qasas Ayah 13 the mother of Musa's eyes are cool
  • The Messenger said the coolness of my eyes were placed in my salah

Lower your wings

  • Surah Isra Ayah 24
  • We are being compared to a bird
  • A bird is about to fly when raising its wings
  • A bird is lowering its wings when it is coming back down to the earth
  • So lowering your wings means to come back down to the earth
  • It means to be humble
  • A bird has the ability to fly and raise its wings but it still lowers them
  • Allah is telling the children to lower their wings before their parents
  • Children are completely dependent on the parents when they are younger
  • As children get older the children must remain humble before their parents
  • Allah says I know you have wings but pretend you don't when you are with your parents
  • We should lower our wings because of the love and care our parents showed us
  • Allah reverses the roles as time goes by
  • We should show love and care to our parents so Allah will show love and care to us
  • Lower the wings of the bird is a parent sacrificing itself for the child
  • We protect our parents as they protected us when we were small

Untie the Knot from my tongue

  • Surah Taha Ayah 25
  • A string is jumbled up and you do not know where it ends or where it begins
  • When you tongue has a knot you do not have clarity of speech
  • Unite the knot from my tongue means to get clarity of speech
  • To have a clear beginning, a clear middle, and a clear end
  • Having a knot in the tongue is like having a stutter
