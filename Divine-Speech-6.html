Past Tense and Present Tense

  • The Past Tense is used when stating something done once
  • The Present Tense is used when stating something happening continually
  • In Surah Yusuf Yusuf says to his father I saw a dream
  • So Yusuf saw the dream once
  • In Surah Saffat Ibrahim tells his son I see in a dream
  • So Ibrahim saw the dream more than once over and over again
